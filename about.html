About Me

I started out life in Shanghai, China, where I lived for a whole three days before immigrating with my mother to the US. Then, I moved wherever my father found employment, from Charlottesville, Virginia to the suburbs of Chicago. As a college student, I spend the majority of my time in Philadelphia, Pennslyvania where I attend the University of Pennslyvania, studying Networked and Social Systems Engineering (NETS). Among my many hobbies and interests are literature, rhythm games, music production, hackathons, and eating.
